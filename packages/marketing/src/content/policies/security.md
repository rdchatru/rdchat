If you believe you have found a security issue affecting RdChat, please report it responsibly to `support@rdchat.ru`.

## Safe harbor

If you act in good faith, follow this policy, avoid privacy violations, and do not disrupt the service, RdChat Open Project will not pursue legal action solely for your security research.

## Scope

This policy covers the hosted RdChat websites, clients, APIs, and supporting services operated for the public RdChat service.

Out of scope examples include:

- denial-of-service or load-testing activity;
- social engineering, phishing, or physical attacks;
- reports involving third-party services we do not control;
- purely cosmetic issues with no security impact; and
- testing that accesses data, communities, or accounts you do not own or have permission to use.

## How to report

Email your report to `support@rdchat.ru` and include:

- a short summary of the issue;
- the affected page, endpoint, or client;
- step-by-step reproduction details;
- the impact you believe the issue has; and
- screenshots, logs, or proof-of-concept material if helpful.

Please do not publish the issue before we have had a reasonable opportunity to investigate and, where appropriate, fix it.

## Testing rules

When researching on RdChat:

- test only with accounts and data you control or have explicit permission to use;
- avoid scraping, flooding, brute force, or disruptive automation;
- do not attempt to view, modify, or exfiltrate other users' private data;
- stop testing immediately if you think you may be affecting real users; and
- contact us before any test that could trigger billing, notifications, or service instability.

## What to expect from us

We aim to:

- acknowledge reports within a reasonable time;
- review and triage valid reports as quickly as practical;
- request extra detail if we cannot reproduce the issue; and
- coordinate follow-up or disclosure where appropriate.

## Recognition

We may acknowledge helpful security reports at our discretion, but we do not promise rewards, compensation, or public credit.

Thank you for helping keep RdChat secure.
